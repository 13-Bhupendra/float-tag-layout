Sushant Singh Rajput

About

Sushant Singh Rajput was a talented Bollywood actor known
for his versatility and charm. He gained widespread recognition
with his role in *MS Dhoni: The Untold Story*, portraying
India's cricket legend. His journey from television to becoming
a beloved film star inspired many. Tragically, his untimely death
in 2020 left a deep void in Indian cinema and sparked discussions on mental health.
